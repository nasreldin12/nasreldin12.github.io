About the Author

Nasreldin Yousif

Civil Engineer · BIM-AI Strategist · Founder, NGB Engineering Enterprises

Nasreldin Adam Mohammed Yousif is a civil engineer, BIM coordinator, and digital transformation strategist with over a decade of experience in large-scale infrastructure and building projects across the Middle East and Africa. He is the founder of NGB Engineering Enterprises and a specialist in applying AI-driven BIM workflows to complex construction programmes. His work spans ISO 19650 implementation, machine learning for cost prediction, federated data architectures, and carbon intelligence frameworks. This book is the distillation of ten years of field-level work — not academic conjecture.
